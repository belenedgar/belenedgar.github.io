About Me

I am a Computer Science major at Northwestern University hoping to continue on to grad school, for a PhD in computer science with a focus on education. I am currently an undergraduate TA, helping with the introductory computer science classes at Northwestern. I am also a part of YETI(Youth Education Technology for Inclusivity) Lab, which is attached to TIDAL Lab at Northwestern. This research is focused on constructing educational opportunities for youth to learn about computer science via creative and nonconventional avenues. The current curriculum focuses on the intersection of music and computer science using TunePad, a platform that allows users to create music with Python.
