Title: Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients:
- 1 cup flour
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp butter, melted
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, mix the flour and sugar.
2. Add milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Stir until smooth.
3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium heat.
4. Pour batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
5. Cook until pancakes are golden brown on both sides. Serve hot.
